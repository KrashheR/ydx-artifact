Нельзя объединять изображения в коллаж, сетку, триптих, split-screen или одно общее полотно. Каждая картинка должна быть самостоятельным файлом одинакового размера и формата 16:10. ## Готовый промпт

```text
ABSOLUTE OUTPUT REQUIREMENT — DO NOT IGNORE:
- Generate exactly 3 images as 3 separate image files.
- Image A, Image B and Image C must each be delivered as an individual standalone file.
- Do NOT combine the three images into one canvas.
- Do NOT create a collage, triptych, contact sheet, grid, split screen or side-by-side composition.
- Do NOT place multiple versions inside the same image.
- Each of the 3 files must have the same dimensions and a 16:10 aspect ratio.

Generate 3 separate images in one response for a find-the-difference game scene.

Required outputs:
1. Image A — original.
2. Image B — altered version with exactly 9 declared differences.
3. Image C — original with all differences circled.

CRITICAL:
- 3 separate files;
- no collage;
- same dimensions;
- aspect ratio 16:10;
- identical composition and all unchanged objects;
- no extra differences;
- no text, logos, watermark, UI, captions or visible people.

STYLE:
- premium semi-realistic jungle expedition illustration;
- late-1970s equipment;
- swollen tropical river;
- grounded forensic mystery.

STORY CONTEXT:
The official archive claims a flood destroyed the bridge. The original photograph shows that the support ropes were deliberately cut.

SCENE:
A wooden suspension bridge crossing a swollen jungle tributary. Half of the walkway hangs close to the water. Wet planks, ropes, vines, driftwood and expedition supply cases are visible. The far bank rises toward ancient stone-covered terrain.

KEY STORY CLUE:
The expedition intentionally blocked the route behind itself.

THE EXACT 9 DIFFERENCES IN IMAGE B:
1. Change the cleanly cut left support rope into a naturally frayed rope.
2. Remove the cutting tool lying on the near bank.
3. Restore three missing bridge planks.
4. Remove the rope fibers caught on the metal hook.
5. Change the overturned supply case into an upright closed case.
6. Remove the boot prints leading away from the damaged support.
7. Add a large driftwood log pressed against the bridge.
8. Remove the emergency crossing rope tied above the water.
9. Change the orange waterproof supply bag to dark brown.

SOLUTION IMAGE:
Image C must exactly reproduce Image A and circle all 9 declared difference locations.
```
