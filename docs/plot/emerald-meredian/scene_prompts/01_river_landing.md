# Emerald Meridian — Level 01

## River Landing / Речной причал

> **Критическое требование к генерации:** результат должен содержать **ровно 3 отдельные картинки в 3 отдельных файлах**. Нельзя объединять изображения в коллаж, сетку, триптих, split-screen или одно общее полотно. Каждая картинка должна быть самостоятельным файлом одинакового размера и формата 16:10.

## Готовый промпт

```text
ABSOLUTE OUTPUT REQUIREMENT — DO NOT IGNORE:
- Generate exactly 3 images as 3 separate image files.
- Image A, Image B and Image C must each be delivered as an individual standalone file.
- Do NOT combine the three images into one canvas.
- Do NOT create a collage, triptych, contact sheet, grid, split screen or side-by-side composition.
- Do NOT place multiple versions inside the same image.
- Each of the 3 files must have the same dimensions and a 16:10 aspect ratio.

Generate 3 separate images in one response for a find-the-difference game scene.

Required outputs:
1. Image A — the original unchanged expedition photograph.
2. Image B — the deliberately altered archive version with exactly 9 declared differences.
3. Image C — a solution/reference image based on Image A, with all 9 difference locations marked by thin clear circles.

CRITICAL OUTPUT RULES:
- create 3 separate image files;
- do not combine them into one collage;
- do not place them side by side;
- all three images must have identical dimensions;
- aspect ratio for each image: 16:10;
- Image B must preserve the exact composition, camera, crop, perspective, lighting, weather, textures and all unchanged objects from Image A;
- Image C must be identical to Image A except for the 9 thin solution circles;
- no random extra differences;
- no readable text;
- no logos;
- no brands;
- no watermark;
- no UI;
- no captions;
- no visible people.

GLOBAL STYLE:
- premium semi-realistic illustrated hidden-object scene;
- late-1970s tropical archaeological and botanical expedition;
- warm humid jungle atmosphere;
- grounded non-supernatural mystery;
- all important objects clearly visible;
- polished adventure-game illustration;
- detailed but readable on a mobile screen.

STORY CONTEXT:
The expedition arrives by river. Official records claim the team carried only archaeological equipment, but the original image reveals extensive equipment for testing water contamination.

SCENE:
A wooden expedition landing on a wide muddy-green jungle river. A weathered supply boat is tied to the pier. Canvas-covered crates, fuel drums, rope coils, metal cases and botanical equipment are being unloaded. Dense rainforest rises behind a narrow trail leading inland. The scene is orderly and recently used.

KEY STORY CLUE:
The expedition secretly brought instruments for testing contaminated water.

THE EXACT 9 DIFFERENCES IN IMAGE B:
1. Remove the glass water-sampling bottles from the open wooden crate.
2. Replace the metal water-testing case with a plain canvas bag.
3. Remove the long measuring pole leaning against the pier.
4. Change the red fuel drum on the boat into a wooden barrel.
5. Remove the folded river map from the top of the crate.
6. Replace the two green specimen jars with one empty metal cup.
7. Remove the narrow trail marker tied to the tree at the jungle entrance.
8. Change the white canvas boat canopy to dark green.
9. Remove the portable filtration pump beside the rope coil.

SOLUTION IMAGE:
Image C must reproduce Image A exactly and place one thin clear circle around each of the 9 declared difference locations.
```
